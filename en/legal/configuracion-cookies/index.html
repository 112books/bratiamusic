How to manage cookies

You can configure cookies from your browser. Here’s how to do it in the main browsers:

Google Chrome

  1. Click the menu icon (three vertical dots) in the top right corner
  2. Select “Settings”
  3. Scroll to “Privacy and security” and click “Cookies and other site data”
  4. Select the option you prefer

Chrome cookie settings

Mozilla Firefox

  1. Click the menu icon (three horizontal lines) in the top right corner
  2. Select “Settings”
  3. Go to “Privacy & Security”
  4. In the “Cookies and Site Data” section, choose the desired option

Firefox cookie settings

Safari

  1. Click “Safari” in the top menu bar
  2. Select “Preferences”
  3. Go to the “Privacy” tab
  4. Enable or disable the “Block all cookies” option

Safari cookie settings

Microsoft Edge

  1. Click the menu icon (three horizontal dots) in the top right corner
  2. Select “Settings”
  3. Go to “Privacy, search, and services”
  4. In the “Cookies” section, choose the option you prefer
